School Facilities

Library

The Library is unrivalled with numerous books for student reference and good reading. The library also has Digital content for students of all classes.

Computer Lab

The Computer Lab is equipped with high-speed central servers and the latest educational software. Every student has access to a terminal in the lab.

Medical - First Aid

The School has a first aid facility of its own. Regular medical check-ups for the entire school is to be done routinely.

Transportation Facilities

The school has bus facility that covers the town and nearby areas. Specially designed school buses for the safety & comfort of the students are used.

Welcome to MIGHS Kalina

Mary Immaculate Girls’ High School, Kalina located opposite Our Lady of Egypt church, Kalina is a minority institution affiliated to the Maharashtra State Board of secondary and Higher Secondary Education. It is run by the Mission Sisters of Ajmer, an Indian congregation of religious sisters working for the upliftment of women.

Child centered approach

We place the pupil at the centre of the learning process in classroom. The teacher provides the opportunities to the students to learn independently and ... from one another and coaches them in the skills they need to learn and practice, it inspires kids to want to learn by giving them the steering wheel to their educational path. Individualised learning helps children become more independent. Students learn to direct their fascinations, ask questions, and work on their own as well as part of a group.

Our Parents Say!

Mary Immaculate Girls High School Kalina offers an exceptional environment for girls, combining top-tier education with a strong focus on discipline and safety. The school’s commitment to high academic standards ensures that every student is given the tools to succeed, while fostering a respectful and structured atmosphere. Discipline is taught as a core value, helping students develop responsibility and integrity. Additionally, the safety of the girls is a top priority, with comprehensive security measures and a caring, supportive community.

Mary Immaculate Girls High School Kalina truly provides a balanced and nurturing environment for girls to thrive both academically and personally. Highly recommended!

Shihab Abdul Samath

Mary Immaculate Girls' High School, located in Kalina, is a prestigious institution known for its commitment to academic excellence and holistic development.

The teachers are well-mannered, kind, and always ready to support their students. The school fosters a vibrant environment by celebrating various festivals, hosting competitions, and organizing grand annual functions.

Students are encouraged to showcase their talents through events such as singing, dancing, acting, and other skill-based performances. The school also actively participates in interschool competitions, and its band is recognized as one of the finest.

There is excellent communication and coordination among teachers, students, and parents, ensuring a strong support system for every child’s growth.

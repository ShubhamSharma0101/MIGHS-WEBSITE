Vision Statement of MIGHS Kalina

We, the Sisters of the Congregation of the Mission of Ajmer and our collaborators, inspired by the teachings of Jesus Christ, and the vision of our Founder Bishop Henry Fortunatus Caumont commit ourselves to work for the integral liberation of women and girl children of our society. As women of substance, of compassion and of commitment to service, we will work for the uplift of all, especially for the under privileged of the society. Our focus in this ministry is to envision all “Our staff and students towards service, thus preparing them to be authentic, responsible, just, dynamic and spiritual men and women for others.”

To meet the challenges posed by communalism and globalisation we will promote a counter culture of harmony and solidarity, based on Spirituality of Communion, as a basis for learning to live together, in and through our Institutions.
